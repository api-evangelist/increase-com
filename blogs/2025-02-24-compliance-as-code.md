As an engineer, how do you ensure that a code change doesn’t inadvertently break a compliance or legal obligation? More fundamentally, how do you even know what those obligations are? Conway’s Law tells us that compliance and engineering teams tend to operate in separate systems that rarely talk to each other. As a result, policies, procedures, and legal frameworks live in one world, while production systems live in another.
