Vantaca, a leading platform for community association management, launched Vantaca Vendor to let property managers pay vendors directly from the same system where they manage invoices, approvals, and accounting. The solution is powered by Increase, a modern banking core that helped Vantaca replace a fragmented stack of payments tools with a single integration — launching and scaling in weeks, not months.
