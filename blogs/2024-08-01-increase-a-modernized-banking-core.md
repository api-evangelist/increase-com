A financial institution can maintain its existing core while simultaneously adding a new core. The two cores can interact as necessary (either by exchanging data directly or separately interacting with upstream or downstream services). This is what Increase does: we operate as a parallel core for our bank partners. We also expose functionality of the underlying depository rails via powerful APIs.
