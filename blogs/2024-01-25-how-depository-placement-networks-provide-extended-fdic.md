Increase and our bank partners now offer access to Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (“FDIC”) coverage on deposits of more than $250,000. This works through our connection to a deposit placement network offered by IntraFi. You can place deposits using IntraFi’s ICS service via Increase’s Dashboard or API.
